Welcome to GryphonCTF!

GryphonCTF is a cybersecurity competition organised by Singapore Polytechnic Diploma in Infocomm Security Management's Student Committee. The competition is to be from XX XXX 2016 to XX XXX 2016. This competition hopes to improve the technical skill sets and logical thinking of DISM students across levels.

Competition Details

This competition is targeted at Singapore Polytechnic Diploma in Infocomm Security Management. However, other courses and schools are allowed to participate so long as the organisers allow it. All participants are required to form a team of 4 members and are given a shared account during the competition period. Participants are allowed to use their own equipment and tools so long as they adhere to the rules.

This competition's format will be Capture the Flag, which means that teams must solve questions and find the flag (usually text) and upload to the official scoreboard website. The team with the highest points at the end of the competition will be the winner. If there is a tie for first place, the team which scores the most points first will get the first place.

More information regarding the competition will be released either through this website, or via the emails provided by the teams as the date draws close to the competition.

Rules

  • Do not attack the PCs of other competitors.
  • Do not attack the scoring server.
  • Do not attack anything outside of the CTF environment.
  • Do not delete or modify flags.
  • ARP poisoning or any form of man-in-the-middle attack is prohibited. This action is subject to disqualification.
  • Sharing flags or solutions is prohibited. This action is subject to disqualification.
  • Brute-forcing flags will not be tolerated.
  • Denial of service attacks on any challenge will not be tolerated, unless the challenge calls for such an attack.
  • If you find any bug in the system, let us know. We might give you some bonus points.
  • Organizers' decision is final.
